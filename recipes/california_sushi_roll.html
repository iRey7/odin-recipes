California Sushi Roll

Rolled white rice with a delicious center

Rolled up rice either with or without the seaweed and stuff the center with either meat or vegtables.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • ½ cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 sheets nori (dry seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced lengthwise
  • 2 sticks imitation crab, sliced lengthwise
